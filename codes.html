Resources

Here you will find infromation, data sources, relevant links and codes in my Github repository on topics related to ocean science.

Data

NOAA, DART real-time tsunami monitoring systems, provide wave elevation data, in this site you can download time series related to tsunami events NOAA

Earthquakes, USGS catalog of earthquakes contitutes a database with magnitude and source properties of earthquakes all around the globe USGS

Earthquakes, Global harvard CMT moment tensor also provides an extensive earthquakes database CMT

Ocean Networks Canada provides data about underwater sounds recorded by their network of hydrophones Ocean Networks Canada

Codes

Tide and wave data

NOAA provides water level data, and wind and atmospheric pressure recorded by their tide gauges deployed along the U.S. coasts at NOAA

CDIP short period wave spectra can be accessed at CDIP

Topobathymetric data

NOAA satellite imagery, land cover and topobathymetric data at diverse resolutions at NOAA

GEBCO provides medium to low resolution bathymetric data worldwide GEBCO

Climate data

ERA5 from the Copernicus network provides multiresolution data of many variables with worldwide coverage at different periods of time ERA5
